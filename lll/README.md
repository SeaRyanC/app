## LLL - Little League Lineup

A game scheduling tool for Little League baseball coaches. Enter your roster, mark who's present, and configure which positions each player is eligible for — then generate a fair, randomized lineup for the whole game with one click.

The scheduler distributes playing time equitably using a round-robin system: no player repeats a position until everyone else eligible has played it (including bench time). It also tries to alternate players between high-intensity positions (Pitcher, Catcher, 1st Base, 2nd Base) and lower-intensity ones to keep things fresh and fair. Your roster and settings are saved automatically so you're always ready for the next game.
